Comforting Tomato Soup

  • 6 large ripe tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or coconut milk for a dairy-free option)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Roast the Tomatoes & Garlic:

    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Place the halved tomatoes and garlic cloves on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
    • Roast for 25-30 minutes until the tomatoes are caramelized and tender.

  2. Sauté the Onion:

    • In a large pot, heat a little olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and translucent (about 5 minutes).

  3. Blend the Soup:

    • Transfer the roasted tomatoes, garlic, and sautéed onions to a blender. Add the vegetable broth and blend until smooth.
    • If you prefer a chunkier texture, blend partially.

  4. Simmer & Season:

    • Pour the blended soup back into the pot.
    • Stir in the heavy cream (or coconut milk), balsamic vinegar, and fresh basil. Simmer for 10 minutes on low heat.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

  5. Serve & Enjoy:

    • Ladle into bowls and garnish with extra basil leaves and a drizzle of cream.
    • Serve with crusty bread or a grilled cheese sandwich for the ultimate comfort meal!
